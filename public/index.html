Benefits of Green Roofs to Cities

1.) Direct Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Reductions

Plants can sequester greenhouse gas emissions from rooftop gardens

2.) Green Roofs Reduce Heating/Cooling GHGs

Heating and Cooling can account for 50% of a city's greenhouse gas emissions. Green roofs reduce this need by providing effective insulation.

3.) Green Roofs Support Biodiversity and Urban Agriculture

Urban wildlife can take shelter on green roofs while providing opportunities for citizens to engage with urban agriculture.


Source: City of Vancouver (2018)
